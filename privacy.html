Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Christians Global is operated by Ian Ridpath, trading as Christians Global.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or interact with our community. We are committed to transparency and to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary for the operation of Christians Global:

  • Website Contact Forms: If you use our contact or prayer forms, we collect the name, email address, and message you provide.
  • Standard Server Logs: Our hosting providers may collect standard server logs (such as IP addresses and browser types) necessary for technical operation, security, and troubleshooting.

2. Data Commitments

We believe in responsible stewardship of information. As a core commitment to our community, we guarantee:

  • We do not sell personal data to any third party.
  • We do not scrape private social media data.
  • We do not operate direct message spam, follower manipulation, mass liking, or mass commenting tools.

3. Future Authorised Social Connections

We are developing responsible internal tools to help us prepare and manage original content for Christians Global owned social accounts. If and when a future authorised social platform connection is established, the following data may be processed strictly for authorised publishing:

  • Account identifiers and authorised access credentials.
  • Content supplied specifically for publication.
  • Publication records and limited post-performance data, where permitted by the platform.

4. Data Retention

We only retain data for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. Our retention approach is as follows:

  • Website contact and support messages: Retained for up to 12 months after the matter is resolved.
  • Privacy, data rights, safeguarding, copyright or formal complaint correspondence: Retained for up to 3 years after resolution, unless a longer period is required for a legal claim or legal obligation.
  • Future authorised social account connection records and publication logs: Retained while the connection or content operations record is needed, then deleted or anonymised within 24 months of the connection ending or the record no longer being needed.
  • Future platform access credentials: Retained only while an account connection remains authorised. Removed promptly when authorisation is withdrawn or the connection is no longer required.
  • Technical hosting and security logs: Retained only for the period necessary for security, troubleshooting and service operation.

Retention periods may be reviewed and updated if our actual services or legal requirements change.

5. Service Providers and Tools

We may use carefully selected service providers to host our website, provide email, operate internal workflow tools, maintain internal content records and help create original content. We only use providers where necessary for the relevant purpose. Currently, these include:

  • Manus, for website creation and hosting.
  • GoDaddy, for domain registration and DNS management.
  • Google Workspace or Gmail, for the monitored support email.
  • DigitalOcean, for hosting our internal n8n automation environment.
  • n8n, for internal workflow automation.
  • Google Sheets, for internal content planning and operational records.
  • OpenAI, for internal content generation and adaptation.

We are independent and not affiliated with, nor do we currently process data with, platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, X, Meta, or Bluesky, unless formally confirmed here.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website is built to prioritise your privacy. We currently use only strictly necessary technical cookies, if any, required for the website to function securely. We do not use non-essential cookies, analytics, tracking scripts, or embedded advertising trackers.

If we introduce analytics or non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and provide a clear cookie consent notice.

7. Data Rights, Access, and Deletion

You have the right to understand and control your data. You may request to:

  • Access your data.
  • Correct any inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data.
  • Withdraw permission for any connected social account (if applicable).

To make a request, or if you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us directly at our monitored support email: christiansglobalcommunity@gmail.com.

8. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.
